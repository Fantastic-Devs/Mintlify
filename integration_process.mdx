After the NDA is signed your ACT Integration Team will send you a welcome email with credentials, allowing you to access the ACT Integration Portal.   This contains all the integration documentation and API Playground required to complete the integration. 

To sign up for a Sandbox Account, you must complete the request form on the ACT Integration Portal.

Start 2-week integration supported by ACT Integration Team 

We now enter a two-week integration period. 

You will be sent details of the SLACK channel that has been created specifically for communication through the 2-week integration. 
 

Support Plan 

During the ACT Geosafe Integration process technical support will be provided.  The goal is to ensure a seamless integration process to resolve questions efficiently and in a timely manner.  

Integration Support Structure 

Level 1 Support (ACT Integration Team) 
Method of contact (JIRA tickets, email, Slack) 

Integration complete Customer is booked in for a pre-live certification with Fantastic 

The pre-live certification is a meeting coordinated by the ACT Account Manager. Technical staff from ACT will be present to check the Sandbox database and logs to ensure a successful integration. Test plan for certification will be provided and completed. 

The required ACT staff on the meeting are Account Manager, DBA, SDK developer 

Production Key is sent to Customer 

Once the certification process has been successfully completed, we can create your account in the Production database and provide your Production API Key.
